Juicy Beyond Burger Recipe

A simple, delicious plant-based burger using the Beyond Burger patty.

Recipe

Ingredients (for 2 burgers)

  • 2 Beyond Burger patties
  • 2 burger buns (preferably toasted)
  • 2 slices vegan cheese (optional)
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 4 tomato slices
  • 4–6 pickle slices
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp vegan mayo
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prep the toppings

    Wash and dry the lettuce. Slice the tomato, red onion, and pickles. Set aside.

  2. Toast the buns

    Lightly toast the burger buns in a dry pan or toaster until golden and slightly crispy.

  3. Cook the Beyond Burger patties

    Heat the cooking oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add the Beyond Burger patties and cook for about 4–5 minutes per side, until nicely browned. Season lightly with salt and pepper. If using vegan cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.

  4. Mix the sauce

    In a small bowl, mix the vegan mayo, ketchup, and mustard. Taste and adjust to your liking.

  5. Assemble the burgers

    Spread the sauce on the bottom half of each bun. Add lettuce, tomato slices, and pickles. Place the cooked Beyond Burger patty (with melted vegan cheese if using) on top. Add onion slices and close with the top bun.

  6. Serve & enjoy

    Serve immediately with your favorite sides and get ready to share your creation!
